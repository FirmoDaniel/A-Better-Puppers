First thing's first, let's get the food facts straight.

  • Scarps don't count, it's all about the protein.
  • Leading brand ain't always the best.
  • Lots of factors dictate a dog's dietery needs, not just age and gender.
  • Extra excercise doesn't mean extra food.
  • Knock 10% off their food if you're heavy with the treats
  • 60% in the morning, and 40% in the evening is best.
  • The more protein in the food, the less food they need.

I'm sure we don't need to go on and on about the benefits of a healthy diet. We're pretty sure you didn't come here for a lecture, so let's get to it. Check out our feeding guide to help you on your way.
